# EDA Notes

## Key Findings

* Temperature shows a positive relationship with yield.
* Humidity values are concentrated within a narrow range.
* CO₂ shows a weak negative relationship with yield.
* Yield values remain relatively stable throughout the dataset.

## Correlation Summary

* Strongest positive correlation: Temperature and Yield.
* Strongest negative correlation: CO₂ and Yield.
* Correlation does not imply causation; other environmental factors may also affect yield.
